Our Journey

Company History

2017

The Beginning

Wanderlust was founded by Marcus Chen and Amara Osei with a simple blog featuring 10 hand-picked destinations and a vision to democratize travel discovery.

2019

Platform Launch

The interactive web platform launched with dynamic search functionality, reaching 10,000 users within the first month. Our Beach & Temple categories debuted to widespread acclaim.

2021

Global Expansion

Partnerships with local tourism boards across 40 countries enabled us to offer authentic, hyperlocal recommendations. We surpassed 25,000 active monthly users.

2023

AI-Powered Curation

We integrated intelligent search and personalization, transforming how travelers discover destinations. Wanderlust was recognized as one of the top travel platforms globally.

2025

Today & Beyond

With 50,000+ happy travelers and 150+ curated destinations, we continue to innovate, inspire, and connect the world's explorers with extraordinary experiences.
